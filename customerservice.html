Shipping & Returns

Shipping information Norway:
Service: Bring (DHL Express available for additional cost)
Delivery: 1-3 days *

Kindly note that delivery time commence from the date of dispatch
We ship your order no later than the day after the order is received.

Please note that we are not able to deliver to P.O Box addresses.

Delivery times are provided only as guidelines, and does not take into account possible delays caused by payment authorization or stock availability.
The order has to be above the amount of 500 NOK to have free shipping. If the order is below this amount, the shipping cost is 69 NOK.
